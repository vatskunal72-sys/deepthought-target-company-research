## Assignment Overview

This repository contains my submission for the Business Analytics Target Company Research assignment.

The objective of this project was to identify and evaluate manufacturing companies that closely match DeepThought's "Federer Profile" using the qualification framework provided in the assignment.

## Research Summary

* **Target City:** Hyderabad
* **Primary Segments:**

  * Aerospace & Defence Manufacturing
  * Precision Engineering
  * Medical Devices
  * Industrial Electronics

Companies were shortlisted after applying the E1 and E2 qualification criteria and then evaluated using the C3–C8 Federer scoring framework.

---

## Research Sources

The research was conducted using publicly available sources, including:

* Official Company Websites
* LinkedIn Company Pages
* Annual Reports
* Careers Pages
* Public News Articles
* Business Directories (where applicable)

---

## Approach

1. Identified manufacturing companies operating in Hyderabad.
2. Filtered companies using the E1 and E2 qualification criteria.
3. Evaluated each company using the Federer scoring framework (C3–C8).
4. Assigned a final score and verdict based on publicly available evidence.
5. Compiled the final dataset in spreadsheet and CSV format.
