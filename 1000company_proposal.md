# 1000-Company ICP Qualification Proposal

## Objective

The objective is to identify 1,000 companies that genuinely match DeepThought's Federer Profile within one month while maintaining high data quality and minimizing false positives.

---

## Phase 1 – Build the Initial Universe

I would collect an initial list of approximately 8,000–10,000 manufacturing companies from multiple sources:

* MCA database
* Tofler
* Tracxn
* LinkedIn
* IndiaMART
* Startup India
* Industry associations (CII, FICCI, EEPC, ACMA)
* Trade exhibition exhibitor lists
* Government procurement databases

Using multiple sources reduces the risk of missing qualified companies.

---

## Phase 2 – Automated Qualification

Using AI, APIs, and web scraping tools, I would automatically collect:

* Company website
* Manufacturing locations
* Product portfolio
* Leadership information
* Industry segment
* Hiring activity
* News articles
* Expansion announcements
* Certifications

Rule-based filters would automatically eliminate companies that:

* Are service-only businesses
* Are distributors or traders
* Have insufficient public information
* Operate outside target industries

---

## Phase 3 – Federer Scoring

For companies passing E1 and E2:

AI-assisted scoring models would evaluate:

* Product differentiation
* Leadership quality
* Industry attractiveness
* Growth signals
* Operational maturity
* Organizational depth

Each score would require supporting evidence extracted from public sources.

---

## Phase 4 – Quality Control

Quality would be maintained through:

* Cross-checking information across multiple sources
* Manual review of borderline companies
* Duplicate detection
* Random sampling audits
* Evidence verification for every score

Borderline cases would always be manually reviewed before inclusion.

---

## Success Metrics

The project would prioritize quality over quantity.

Every company included in the final dataset would:

* Manufacture physical products.
* Match the ICP profile.
* Have evidence supporting every qualification score.
* Be manually reviewed if classified as a borderline case.
